Past

Technology in the past was quite limited and different from current times. In the mid-90s, there were still landline phones, floppy disks, fax machines, VHS tapes, cassette tapes, and programming itself was very different from what it is today, with limited tools and resources and a much slower and manual development process. The first computer in the world was created in 1946, the ENIAC, which weighed 30 tons, was 5.50 meters tall and 25 meters long, and occupied 180 square meters of floor space. When the ENIAC was first used, the power consumption was so high that the lights in Philadelphia flickered!

Present

Nowadays technology has advanced to the point of being present in practically everything, things that were not even imagined as artificial intelligence that is currently used in various ways are increasingly in the spotlight, even Virtual Reality projects where the environment is replaced from the world real for a simulated are already more and more present.

Future

In the future there will be virtual cities called “The metaverse”, where people can access any place in the world from inside their own homes, all you have to do is put on VR glasses and some sort of equipment that can monitor your movement and with the help of a controller you can access virtual stores and make purchases that will arrive at your home, or even meet a distant friend and talk to him or her with greater emersion.
